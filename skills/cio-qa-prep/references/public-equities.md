# CIO/PM Q&A Prep — Public Equities

## Question Categories

### Thesis & Variant Perception
- "Walk me through your variant perception in one sentence."
- "What's the sell-side consensus and why are they wrong?"
- "Who's on the other side of this trade and why might they be right?"
- "What would change your mind?"

### Valuation & Upside/Downside
- "How did you arrive at your target price?"
- "What multiple are you assigning and why is that justified?"
- "What's priced in at the current level?"
- "Walk me through the sensitivity — what breaks the thesis?"

### Timing & Catalysts
- "Why now? What's the catalyst in the next 3-6 months?"
- "What if the catalyst doesn't materialize on your timeline?"
- "Is this dead money until [event]?"
- "What's the implied move on the next earnings print?"

### Risk & Downside
- "What's the max drawdown scenario and what's the probability?"
- "How does this behave in a risk-off / recession?"
- "What's the short thesis and why is it wrong?"
- "What's the scenario where this costs us our year?"

### Portfolio Construction & Sizing
- "Why this size? What's the Kelly criterion suggest?"
- "How does this correlate with [existing position]?"
- "What's the beta-adjusted exposure?"
- "What would you hedge this with?"
- "Why is this better than adding to [higher conviction position]?"

### Meta / Process
- "What's your informational edge here — primary research, data, model?"
- "How many names have you looked at before landing on this one?"
- "What's the liquidity profile if we need to exit quickly?"

## Killer Questions (Equity-Specific)

- "The sell-side already has this at Overweight with a higher target — where's our edge over their analysts who have management access?"
- "This screens well on your model, but so did [recent loss] — what's structurally different?"
- "If you're right on the thesis, why hasn't a strategic acquirer already moved?"
