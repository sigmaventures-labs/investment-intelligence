# CIO/PM Q&A Prep — Private Equity

## Question Categories

### Sourcing & Price
- "How did we find this deal? Is it proprietary or broadly marketed?"
- "How many bidders are in the process and what are we competing against?"
- "Walk me through the entry multiple — how does it compare to precedent transactions?"
- "Why is the seller selling and what do they know that we don't?"
- "What's our walk-away price?"

### Value Creation Plan
- "Which value creation lever contributes the most to returns? How confident are you in it?"
- "You're underwriting 200bps of margin expansion — where specifically does it come from?"
- "What's the organic revenue growth assumption and what evidence supports it?"
- "How many bolt-ons are in the plan and what happens to returns if you do zero?"
- "Have you spoken to operating partners about the feasibility of the ops plan?"

### Management & Team
- "Is the management team staying? What's the retention plan?"
- "What are the gaps in the management team and who fills them?"
- "How much is management rolling over and is the incentive structure right?"
- "What's the key person risk?"

### Market & Competitive Position
- "What's the sustainable competitive advantage of this business?"
- "Who are the top 3 competitors and why won't they erode margins?"
- "How cyclical is this business and where are we in the cycle?"
- "What's the customer concentration and what happens if the top customer leaves?"

### Capital Structure & Financing
- "Walk me through the sources and uses."
- "What's the leverage at entry and is the debt package committed?"
- "What are the covenants and how much headroom is there?"
- "What's the refinancing risk and when does the debt mature vs. planned exit?"

### Exit
- "Who buys this at your exit multiple and why?"
- "Why would the exit multiple be higher than entry?"
- "What's the IPO path look like and is the business IPO-ready?"
- "What are the returns if we exit at the entry multiple with no expansion?"
- "What if the exit market is closed — can we extend and generate returns from cash flow?"

### Portfolio & Fund Impact
- "How much of the fund is this and does the sizing match the risk?"
- "How does this correlate with our existing portfolio companies?"
- "Does this advance our sector thesis or is it a one-off?"
- "What's the impact on fund returns if this goes to zero?"

## Killer Questions (PE-Specific)

- "You're paying 12x EBITDA for a business growing 5% — walk me through why this isn't a mediocre asset at a full price with a hope-and-pray operational plan."
- "The last three deals in this sector traded at 10-14x and none of them hit their underwritten returns — why is this time different?"
- "You're underwriting a 3.0x MOIC but 40% of the return comes from multiple expansion — isn't that just a bet on the exit market rather than value creation?"
- "If we can't execute the bolt-on strategy, this is a 1.5x MOIC on a 5-year hold — does that clear our hurdle for the risk we're taking?"
