# CIO/PM Q&A Prep — Real Estate

## Question Categories

### Basis & Valuation
- "Walk me through how you got to this basis. What are you paying per SF/unit?"
- "What are the cap rate comps and why do you deserve a discount/premium?"
- "What's replacement cost and how does our basis compare?"
- "If we marked this to market today with a 50bps cap rate move, what's the write-down?"

### Business Plan & Value Creation
- "What's the lease-up timeline and what evidence supports that absorption rate?"
- "What are comparable rents and why can we achieve a premium?"
- "Walk me through the capex budget — what's the contingency and is it enough?"
- "What happens if the renovation takes 6 months longer than planned?"
- "Have you stress-tested the rent growth assumption against the supply pipeline?"

### Tenancy & Leasing Risk
- "What's the concentration risk? What happens if [largest tenant] doesn't renew?"
- "What's your mark-to-market analysis on the rent roll?"
- "How much near-term lease rollover is there and what's your retention assumption?"
- "What's the tenant credit quality and how did you diligence it?"

### Financing & Capital Structure
- "What are the debt terms and how competitive were they?"
- "What's the refinancing risk at maturity? Walk me through the refi scenario."
- "What's the DSCR at stabilization vs. day one?"
- "What happens to returns if rates are 100bps higher at refinancing?"

### Market & Macro
- "What's the supply pipeline in this submarket over the next 3 years?"
- "What are the demand drivers and how cyclical are they?"
- "How does this market perform in a recession? What happened in 2008-2009?"
- "Is this a bet on the market or a bet on the asset?"

### Exit & Returns
- "What exit cap rate are you assuming and what justifies it?"
- "Who's the buyer at exit and why would they pay that price?"
- "What's the levered return if exit cap rates widen 50bps?"
- "Can we generate an acceptable return just from cash flow if the exit market is bad?"

### Portfolio Fit
- "How does this fit with our existing geographic/property type exposure?"
- "Are we overweight this submarket or property type?"
- "What's the opportunity cost vs. the other deals in the pipeline?"

## Killer Questions (RE-Specific)

- "The cap rate looks attractive, but isn't this a value trap — what if the market is correctly pricing in secular demand decline for this property type?"
- "Your business plan assumes you can lease up 30,000 SF in 18 months in a submarket with negative absorption — show me the tenant pipeline."
- "We underwrote [similar deal] two years ago with the same rent growth assumptions and missed by 200bps — what's different this time?"
