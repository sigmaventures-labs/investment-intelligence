# Cross-Asset Signals — Public Equities

## Equity-Specific Signal Layer

In addition to the core five lenses, equity analysts should monitor:

### Equity Market Internals
- **Breadth**: Advance/decline lines, % of stocks above 200-day MA — divergence
  from index = fragile rally
- **Sector rotation**: Where is leadership? Defensive vs. cyclical rotation signals
- **Factor performance**: Growth vs. value, momentum, quality — regime indicator
- **Small cap vs. large cap**: Small cap underperformance often leads broader weakness

### Earnings Signal
- **Earnings revision breadth**: % of companies with upward vs. downward revisions
- **Guidance trends**: Net positive vs. negative guidance across S&P 500
- **Earnings surprise trajectory**: Are beats shrinking or expanding?

### Fund Flows & Positioning
- **Equity fund flows**: ETF vs. mutual fund, domestic vs. international
- **Hedge fund gross/net exposure** (prime broker surveys)
- **Buyback activity**: Corporate demand as a flow signal
- **Margin debt levels**: Excessive leverage = fragility

## Equity-Specific Playbooks

- **Credit tightening + earnings revisions negative + small cap underperformance**
  → Broad equity risk-off imminent
- **VIX backwardation + put skew elevated + fund positioning net long**
  → Market bracing for a correction but positioned for it; may resolve sideways
- **IG spreads tight + equity vol low + IPO/M&A pipeline heavy**
  → Late-cycle exuberance; monitor for cracks
- **Copper rising + earnings revisions positive + curve steepening**
  → Cyclical recovery; rotate from defensives to cyclicals
- **Dollar weakening + EM equities outperforming + commodity strength**
  → Global growth re-acceleration; broaden geographic exposure
