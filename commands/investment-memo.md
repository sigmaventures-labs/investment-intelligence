# Investment Memo

Generate a professional investment memo for:

$ARGUMENTS

Refer to @.cursor/skills/investment-memo/SKILL.md for the core principles, then load the public equities template: @.cursor/skills/investment-memo/references/public-equities.md

Follow the asset-class-specific memo template exactly. Every memo must include: deal/position summary header, executive summary, variant perception/edge, asset/business overview, key drivers/value creation, path to value realization, valuation/returns with sensitivity, risk factors with mitigants, sizing/deployment, and monitoring framework.

Writing principles: argue don't describe, quantify everything, steel-man the counter, time-bound the thesis, source the edge. Every paragraph must earn its place.

Note: Real estate and private equity overlays are in development and will be available in a future release.
